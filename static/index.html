About

Who I Am

I am an Advocate by profession, presently practicing before the High Court of Kerala and associated with Nagaraj Associates, Empire Building. The office is headed by Sri. Nagaraj Narayan, Public Prosecutor for the Forest Department and Director of Law Academy Law College, Thiruvananthapuram, and is a well-established and reputed legal office.

Along with my High Court practice, I also have an affiliated lower court office at Pala, namely Jose Anakalingal, and I actively practice before the courts at Pala and independently at Thodupuzha.

I have written and published several legal articles in reputed journals, including the Kerala High Court Cases (KHC) Journal. I am also a writer, with a strong interest in legal research, academics, and contemporary legal issues.
